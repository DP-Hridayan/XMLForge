<p><i>XMLForge</i> is a simple and powerful XML formatter & attribute reordering tool for Android.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Formats XML with proper indentation</li><li>Reorders attributes in a structured way</li><li>Supports Android XML files</li><li>One-click copy to clipboard</li><li>Simple and intuitive UI</li></ul><p><b>How It Works</b></p><ol><li>Paste or load your XML content.</li><li>Tap the Format button to clean up the structure.</li><li>View the formatted output with attributes ordered correctly.</li><li>Copy the formatted XML with a single tap.</li></ol>
